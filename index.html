This is a Fake News Website. This website is to inform you all about fake news, what it is, and how to avoid it by knowing what it generally looks like and where to find factual news.

What is Fake News?

Fake news can either be news that is deliberately published to get people to believe something that is not true or news that has some truth to it but does not tell the whole story.

Why is it important?

One main reason is that it can make people believe things that are completely untrue. In addition, it can make people have less trust in the media, as well as make everyone believe something that might be inaccurate.
